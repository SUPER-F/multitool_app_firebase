Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update the app?

Please open the App Store, click on the profile icon in the top right corner to enter the account page, pull down to refresh. If there are available updates, the app will be listed under updates. Click the "Update" button to complete the update.

What should I do if some features are not working properly?

Please try the following solutions first:

  1. Ensure your device is connected to the internet
  2. Restart the app
  3. Check if it's the latest version
  4. Restart your device

If the problem persists, please contact us and provide detailed error information and screenshots.
